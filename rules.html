General Rules And Regulations

  1. Only bonafide, full time student, who is enrolled under University for-
    • Junior College OR
    • Diploma Programme OR
    • Under Graduate Programme OR
    • Post-graduate Degree Programme.
  2. Casual students, external students and students pursuing only bridge course in Affiliated College / Constituent College / Recognized Institute / Centre / Academic Department of University are not eligible.
  3. Student enrolled in distance education programme of the University is not eligible.
  4. Students having provisional admission in Affiliated College / Constituent College / Recognized Institute / Centre / Academic Department of University are not eligible.
  5. Student can represent only that Affiliated College / Constituent College / Recognized Institute / Centre / Academic Department of University from where he / she is pursuing his / her degree / diploma.
  6. Student should have valid PRN No. / Registration No., Identity Card and Fees Receipt of the Affiliated College / Constituent College / Recognized Institute / Centre / Academic Department of University from where he / she is pursuing his / her degree / diploma.
