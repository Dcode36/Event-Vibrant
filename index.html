ABOUT US

About Vibrant

A cultural event named "Vibrant" is a celebration of diverse cultural experiences and traditions. This event is characterized by lively and energetic atmosphere, showcasing the richness of cultural heritage through music, dance, food, art and other forms of expression. The aim of this event is to bring people together, foster understanding, and promote unity through a shared appreciation of cultural diversity. "Vibrant" promises to be a colorful and exciting experience for all attendees.

About SGU

The Sanjay Ghodawat University stands as a beacon of light to guide the younger generation of the day on the right path to fulfilment in career and life. The USP of the University is its research based curriculum and academically oriented teaching staff. The world class ambience and infrastructure helps the students to easily accommodate themselves in an environment that is conducive to the teaching- learning process.

Events

Fashion Show

A fashion show is an event where clothing and accessory collections are displayed on runway models to showcase the latest styles and designs to an audience.

Singing

Singing is the act of producing musical sounds with the voice. It is an activity that can be enjoyed by people of all ages and backgrounds.

Treasure Hunt

A Treasure Hunt is an activity in which participants search for hidden objects or items, typically following a series of clues or riddles. It is often used as a fun and entertaining game, and can be played by people of all ages.

Dance

Dance is a form of artistic expression and physical activity in which individuals or groups move their bodies rhythmically and synchronously to music, often following a set of predetermined steps and patterns.

On spot Painting

On-spot painting is a form of live art where an artist creates a painting in real-time, usually in front of an audience. It is a fast-paced and dynamic process that showcases the artist's skills and creativity.

Reel Making

Reel making refers to the process of creating a compilation or montage of scenes, shots, or clips from a film, video, or other audiovisual material.
